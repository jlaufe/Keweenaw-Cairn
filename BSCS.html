Come in to the studio to create your own one-of-a-kind masterpiece. Pick from a wide variety of bare ceramic pieces, paint it yourself, and when you are done we fire it in our kiln. Or you can start from scratch. Take a lump of clay to make whatever you want. You mold it, shape it, and turn it into your very own.

Not sure if pottery is your thing? The studio offers other options to create your own masterpiece. Try your hand at mosaics or glass fusing. Want to try a more traditional medium? You can get a canvas and paint you own Mona Lisa (or Dogs Playing Poker !).

From beginners to experienced artists, Broad Street Ceramic Studio has something for everyone. If you have never tried something or don't consider yourself an artist, it's OK. We will walk you through everything. We can help you pick a project, show you what to do, and even help you figure out what elements to use to make your finished piece look its best. But in the end it is still your one-of-a-kind masterpiece!
